About KUNDE ELECTRIC

Your trusted partner in power fittings and transmission equipment

Leading Power Fittings Manufacturer

Yiwu Kunde Electric Co., Ltd. Established in 2015 and located in Yiwu — China's world-famous capital of small commodities — our company is a professional exporter specializing in power electric fittings and cable accessories.

With years of steady market development and rich trade accumulation, our products are mainly exported to Southeast Asia, the Middle East, Africa, South America and other global regions.

Adhering to the core philosophy of "Credibility as the Cornerstone, Quality as the Foundation", we strive to deliver high-quality procurement experiences for every partner customer.

Moving forward, we will keep making steady contributions to the sustainable prosperity and development of the global power industry.
