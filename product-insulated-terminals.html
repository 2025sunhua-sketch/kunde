PVC & Nylon Pre-insulated Wire Connectors for Secure Electrical Connections

Insulated Terminals (PVC/Nylon)

Yiwu Kunde Electric Co., Ltd. manufactures high-quality insulated terminals with PVC or nylon insulation. Our pre-insulated wire connectors provide reliable, safe, and efficient electrical connections for control panels, electrical cabinets, automation systems, and various industrial applications.

Key Features

  • Insulation Material: Premium PVC or nylon for excellent electrical insulation
  • Color-Coded: Red (1.5-2.5mm²), Blue (4-6mm²), Yellow (10-16mm²) for easy identification
  • Multiple Types: Ring terminals, fork terminals, butt connectors, pin terminals, spade terminals
  • Easy Installation: Simple crimping process with standard tools
  • Durable Construction: Seamless copper barrel for strong crimp and high conductivity
  • Temperature Resistance: Operational from -40°C to +105°C (PVC), -40°C to +150°C (Nylon)
  • RoHS Compliant: Meets international environmental standards
  • Bulk Wholesale: Competitive factory direct pricing for large orders
