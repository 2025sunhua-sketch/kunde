Aluminum Alloy Cable Clamps

Yiwu Kunde Electric Co., Ltd. manufactures high-quality aluminum alloy cable clamps for professional cable fixing and management. Our cable fixing solutions are designed for single or multiple cables, supporting both horizontal and vertical installations in power plants, substations, industrial facilities, and infrastructure projects worldwide.

Key Features

  • High-Strength Material: Premium aluminum alloy for excellent mechanical strength and durability
  • Corrosion-Resistant: Anodized or powder-coated surface for long-lasting protection
  • Versatile Installation: Suitable for single cable or multiple cable configurations
  • Flexible Mounting: Supports horizontal, vertical, and wall-mounted installations
  • High Load Capacity: Designed to support heavy power cables securely
  • Easy Installation: Simple bolt-on design for quick and secure mounting
  • Custom Sizes: Available in various sizes to accommodate different cable diameters
  • ISO 9001 Certified: Manufactured under strict quality management system
