DTL-1 Series Bimetallic Cable Lugs

Copper-Aluminum Friction Welded Terminals for Power Distribution

DTL-1 Bimetallic Cable Lugs (CU/AL)

Yiwu Kunde Electric Co., Ltd. specializes in manufacturing high-quality DTL-1 series bimetallic cable lugs using advanced friction welding technology. Our copper-aluminum terminals provide reliable connections between copper busbars and aluminum cables in power distribution systems.

Key Features

  • Bimetallic Construction: Copper (CU) + Aluminum (AL) combination for optimal conductivity and cost efficiency
  • Friction Welding Technology: Advanced process ensures superior bond strength and low transition resistance
  • Wide Cable Range: Available from 16mm² to 630mm² to meet various application requirements
  • High Voltage Rating: Suitable for systems up to 35kV
  • Temperature Range: Operational from -55°C to +150°C
  • Surface Treatment: Tin-plated option available for enhanced corrosion resistance
  • International Standards: Compliant with IEC, DIN, and other international electrical standards
  • ISO 9001 Certified: Manufactured under strict quality management system
