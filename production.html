Our Production Line

Advanced manufacturing facilities with strict quality control

Manufacturing Excellence

KUNDE ELECTRIC operates a modern 10,000+ square meter manufacturing facility equipped with advanced production equipment and experienced technicians. Our production line is designed to ensure efficient manufacturing while maintaining the highest quality standards.

From raw material inspection to final product testing, every step of our manufacturing process is carefully controlled. We utilize state-of-the-art machinery for die casting, forging, machining, and surface treatment to produce power fittings that meet international standards.

Our production capacity allows us to handle large orders while maintaining quick turnaround times. We continuously invest in equipment upgrades and process improvements to stay at the forefront of manufacturing technology.

Quality Control

Ensuring every product meets the highest standards

Comprehensive Testing

Our quality control laboratory is equipped with advanced testing equipment to perform mechanical, electrical, and environmental tests on all products. Each batch undergoes rigorous inspection before shipment.

Certifications

We maintain ISO 9001 quality management system certification and our products comply with international standards including IEC, ANSI, and DIN. Regular third-party testing ensures ongoing compliance.

Traceability

Complete product traceability from raw materials to finished goods allows us to maintain strict quality control and quickly address any concerns that may arise.
